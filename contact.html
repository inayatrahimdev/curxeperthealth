Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our support hotline.

Do you offer demos for healthcare providers?

Yes! We offer personalized demos for healthcare providers. You can book a demo through our providers page or contact our sales team directly.

What are your business hours?

Our main office is open Monday-Friday 9 AM-6 PM PST, and Saturday 10 AM-4 PM PST. Our support team is available 24/7 for urgent issues.

How can I partner with CureXpert?

We welcome partnerships with healthcare organizations, investors, and NGOs. Please visit our partners page or contact our partnerships team directly.
